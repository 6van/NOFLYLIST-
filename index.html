ABOUT

NOFLYLIST RECORDS & NFL MEDIA IS AN INDIE LABEL AND MEDIA AGENCY RUN BY A GROUP OF FRIENDS, FOUNDED IN 2022. THOUGH STILL IN ITS EARLY STAGES, NOFLYLIST HAS A VISION OF TRANSCENDING ITS ROOTS AS A RECORD LABEL. THE AGENCY AIMS TO SUPPORT AND SHOWCASE A DIVERSE ARRAY OF ARTISTS AND CREATORS, PUSHING THE BOUNDARIES OF GENRE AND FORM. THEIR ROSTER, THOUGH EMERGING, PROMISES A RICH TAPESTRY OF AVANT-GARDE AND INDIE SOUNDS, CAPTURING THE ETHOS OF INNOVATION AND COMMUNITY.

BY COLLABORATING WITH VARIOUS BRANDS AND CREATIVES, NOFLYLIST ASPIRES TO CARVE OUT A UNIQUE SPACE IN THE MUSIC AND MEDIA LANDSCAPE. AS THEY CONTINUE TO GROW, THEIR COMMITMENT TO AUTHENTICITY AND ARTISTIC EXCELLENCE REMAINS AT THE CORE OF THEIR MISSION.
